What are we?

Penny Juice is a 100% blended fruit juice concentrate that is specifically designed for childcare centers, preschools, Head Starts and just about anyone who loves fruit juice! Penny Juice uses only the highest quality fruit juice products available to ensure our products are as tasty and nutritious as possible!

Penny Juice mixes easily with seven parts water to one part juice concentrate. The concentrate does not require refrigeration before or after opening. Penny Juice greatly reduces storage and handling requirements. Distributors provide pre-measured one gallon pitchers at no charge, to assure cost control and drink consistency. Our system is fast and easy to use. Penny Juice products comply with all requirements for subsidy, and nutrition, set forth by the FDA.

No contract required, free nationowide delivery with a one case minimum order. One case of our juice concentrate is enough to make 24 gallons when diluted! Our distributors offer personalized service to each center. Penny Juice is currently available in all 48 states.

The dimensions of our juices vary depending on the flavour, more information can be found on the product page when ordering.
